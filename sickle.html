sickle cell anemia

Sickle cell affects the shape of red blood cells which carry oxygen to all parts of the body

Symptoms

Sickle cell disease can cause symptoms like:

  • Anemia
  • Episodes of pain
  • Swelling of hands and feets
  • Frequent infections
  • Delayed growth and puberty
  • Vision problems

When to see a doctor:

Seek emergency care for the symptoms of stroke, which include:
  1. One-sided paralysis or weakness in the face,arms or legs
  2. Confusion
  3. Difficulty walking or talking
  4. Sudden vision changes
  5. Unexplained numbness
